Who We Are

Green For Us is a non-profit organization that focuses on environmental education and community-led reforestation projects in urban areas.

Mission Statement

  • Focus: At GFU, we focus on environmental education and community-led reforestation projects. We clean litter in surrounding areas with the help of community members, plant new trees, and upkeep existing ones to create a healthier and cleaner community.
  • Characteristics: Our key characteristics include a strong mission, the ability to mobilize support, adaptability, and a commitment to sustainable practices. (Conrardy, 2022)

Vision Statement

  • Focus: To conserve lands and waters so that people and nature can live in greater harmony.
  • Purpose: To increase trees in urban communities and maintain existing ones with the help of community members.
